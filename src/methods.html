My Methods

My approach is customer-centred, highly personalised, and based on a unique blend of expertise drawn from my experiences as both a seasoned business leader and an award-winning choreographer. As a coach and advisor, I help business leaders and their organisations crystallise visions, identify meaningful growth opportunities, innovate, and promote creativity.

As an executive and life coach, I use The Universal Eclectic Coaching approach, which allows me to select the right model and technique depending on the specific circumstances of my client. I am an accredited GCologist and use the GC Index framework in situations requiring measuring natural propensities of business leaders and teams to create an impact

My approach specifically to coaching creativity is based on the belief that creativity is a fundamental human skill. My creativity coaching method (Human AI Creativity Lab) combines design thinking and improvisation theatre techniques.

Movement coaching is based on somatic approach and draws from my lifelong experience as a dancer. I trained as contemporary dancer in world-famous Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance (London, UK) and am adept at a variety of movement styles and techniques, including release, contact improvisation, and partner work.
